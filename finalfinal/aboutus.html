About Us

History

Inspired by the fond memories of Sunday morning pancakes & trivia with his family, Jax Flores opened Flapjax Diner in 1999 in Cambridge, Massachusetts to spread joy to his community. For Jax, opening a diner was about more than just starting a businessâ€“ it was about gathering his community together and creating lasting memories. Now, as a restaurant owner of over two decades and an experienced trivia player, Jax hopes to continue to make connections with his neighbors and spread the joy of breakfast food and fun games

Mission

Today, Flapjax Diner stays true to its origins as independent breakfast diner while adding a novelty trivia twist. Only at Flapjax will you have the opportunity to show off your intellect while enjoying your classic American diner favorites. At Flapjax, we believe in inclusivity, community, and healthy competition. We love watching our customers come back week after week, improving their scores every time. We want to give back to our community and create a safe place that promises fun for families, friends, and groups of all kinds.
